The time will come when you decide that you would burn for the other even if there were no wind.

Even if the world were anoxic and flooded. Even if the rocks upon which sparks once flew disappeared. If your branches never turned to embers, if no one stoked the coals, you would burn.

There is nothing else. No one else.

Thus begins a marriage. Thus begins, I will burn brighter when you dwindle, and we will keep each other warm. We will rage on, together, in the pursuit of persisting love.

Relentlessly tame by decision, for devotion, and not swept up in the deluge of options, opinions, or obligations. None of it is forced. It is the convergence of two forces of nature, forces who are choosing to nurture fate. To nurture fate, and love it.

Forever. Endlessly.
